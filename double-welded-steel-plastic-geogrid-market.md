<p><p>The Double Welded Steel Plastic Geogrid market is segmented into two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids provide strength in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them suitable for applications like road stabilization and soil reinforcement. Uniaxial tension geogrids, on the other hand, are designed primarily to handle loads in one direction, often used in retaining walls and slope stabilization. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on specific engineering requirements and project goals.</p></p>
