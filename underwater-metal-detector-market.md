<p><p>The underwater metal detector market primarily features three types: Pulse Induction (PI), Very Low Frequency (VLF), and others. Pulse Induction detectors are known for their ability to penetrate deep waters and detect metal in highly mineralized environments, making them ideal for treasure hunting in challenging conditions. Very Low Frequency detectors operate at lower frequencies, providing sensitivity to smaller objects and making them suitable for shallower waters. Other types may include multi-frequency and specialty detectors designed for specific underwater tasks.</p></p>
