Single Beam Scanning SONAR provides precise depth measurements along a single path. Synthetic Aperture SONAR enhances image resolution over large areas. Side Scan SONAR captures high-resolution images of underwater objects, aiding in surveys and inspections, crucial for marine research and security.</p></p>
