Conversely, Oil-immersed transformers operate by submerging components in oil, providing excellent cooling and insulation. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications where voltage regulation is crucial, allowing for efficient power distribution and adaptability to varying load conditions while maintaining operational safety.</p></p>
