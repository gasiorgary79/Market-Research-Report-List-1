<p><p>The Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) scanner market is categorized into two main types: Single Side Reading Type and Dual Side Reading Type. Single Side Reading Type scanners process data from one side of a document, making them suitable for simpler applications such as surveys and tests. In contrast, Dual Side Reading Type scanners can capture data from both sides simultaneously, enhancing efficiency for larger volumes of documents, such as assessments and ballots, where time and accuracy are critical.</p></p>
