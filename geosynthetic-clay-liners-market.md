<p><p>The Geosynthetic Clay Liners (GCLs) market comprises three primary types: Nonwoven Geotextile, Woven Geotextile, and Natural Sodium Bentonite. Nonwoven Geotextiles are typically used for flexibility and filtration, providing enhanced water permeability. Woven Geotextiles offer higher tensile strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-load applications. Natural Sodium Bentonite acts as a critical sealing agent due to its swellable properties, effectively preventing leakage in various environmental applications. Together, these types are essential in waste management and environmental protection.</p></p>
