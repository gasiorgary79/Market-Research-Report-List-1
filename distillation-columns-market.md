<p><p>The distillation columns market can be categorized into two main types: batch columns and continuous columns. Batch columns operate in discrete cycles, allowing flexibility in processing different feed stocks, making them ideal for small-scale production or varied compositions. In contrast, continuous columns run non-stop, offering higher efficiency and throughput, suitable for large-scale operations with consistent feed. Both types serve essential roles in industries like chemicals, petrochemicals, and pharmaceuticals, each catering to specific production needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
