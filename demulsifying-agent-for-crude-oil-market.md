<p><strong>The Demulsifying Agent for Crude Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li><li>Water Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Demulsifying agents are crucial in the crude oil market for separating emulsified water from oil. Oil-soluble demulsifying agents work within the oil phase, disrupting water droplets to facilitate separation. They are ideal for high-oil-content emulsions. Conversely, water-soluble demulsifying agents operate in the aqueous phase and are effective in breaking water-in-oil emulsions. Both types enhance oil recovery and processing efficiency, making them essential for refining operations and improving the quality of crude oil products.</p></p>
