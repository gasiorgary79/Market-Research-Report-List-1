<p><p>The foundry equipment market encompasses several key types essential for metal casting processes. Die casting machines facilitate the production of precise metal parts, while centrifugal casting machines utilize rotational forces for even material distribution. Induction furnaces are vital for melting metals efficiently. Moulding machines shape sand or other materials into molds, and coremaking machines create cores for complex internal shapes. Shot blasting machines prepare surfaces by cleaning or finishing casted components, ensuring quality and enhancing performance in various applications.</p></p>
