<p><p>The cardiac arrhythmia monitoring devices market comprises several key types. Implantable cardiac monitors continuously track heart rhythm and provide long-term data. Resting ECG devices record heart activity during a brief assessment, while ECG stress test devices monitor heart response during exercise. Mobile cardiac telemetry monitors offer real-time data transmission for immediate analysis. Holter monitors are portable devices that record heart activity over extended periods, typically 24 to 48 hours, allowing for comprehensive evaluation of arrhythmias in various settings.</p></p>
