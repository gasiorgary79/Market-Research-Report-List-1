<p><p>The cardiac catheter sensor market includes various types of sensors that enhance cardiac diagnosis and treatment. Pressure sensors monitor hemodynamic parameters, providing vital information on blood flow and vascular resistance. Temperature sensors assess core body temperature during procedures, ensuring patient safety. Biosensors facilitate real-time monitoring of biochemical markers and physiological processes. ECG sensors capture electrical activity of the heart for arrhythmia detection. Image sensors are utilized for visualizing cardiac anatomy, aiding in diagnostics and intervention planning.</p></p>
