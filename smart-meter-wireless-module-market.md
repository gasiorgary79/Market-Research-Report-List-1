<p><p>The Smart Meter Wireless Module market primarily features communication technologies like LoRa and NB-IoT. LoRa (Long Range) provides low-power wide-area network capabilities, ideal for remote rural deployments, enabling long-range data transmission with minimal energy consumption. In contrast, NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT) focuses on high-density urban environments, offering reliable connectivity for a large number of devices with enhanced coverage. Both technologies facilitate efficient data collection and monitoring, enhancing smart grid operations and energy management solutions.</p></p>
