<p><p>The programmable syringe pump market is segmented into three main types: single syringe, dual syringes, and multiple syringes. Single syringe pumps are designed for straightforward applications requiring a single fluid delivery line. Dual syringe pumps allow for simultaneous dispensing of two different fluids, enhancing versatility in treatment. Multiple syringe pumps can support several syringes, making them ideal for complex therapies that require precise control and the administration of multiple medications or fluids at once, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
