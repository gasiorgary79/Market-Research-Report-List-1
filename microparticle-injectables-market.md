<p><p>The microparticle injectables market is categorized into degradable and non-degradable types. Degradable microparticles are designed to break down over time within the body, releasing therapeutic agents gradually, which makes them suitable for sustained drug delivery and reduced frequency of injections. In contrast, non-degradable microparticles remain intact and provide longer-lasting effects, but may require eventual removal or management. Both categories cater to various medical applications, including vaccines, cancer therapies, and sustained release formulations, enhancing treatment efficacy and patient compliance.</p></p>
