<p><p>The hydraulic impact wrench market comprises various tools designed for high-torque applications, primarily categorized into impact wrenches and pulse pneumatic wrenches. Impact wrenches deliver high torque through a hammering mechanism, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks, while pulse pneumatic wrenches utilize bursts of compressed air for precise tightening and loosening, minimizing wear on fasteners. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, construction, and maintenance, where efficiency and power are crucial for fastener applications.</p></p>
