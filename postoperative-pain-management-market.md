<p><p>Postoperative pain management encompasses three main market types: acute, moderate, and severe pain management. Acute pain management focuses on immediate, short-term relief following surgery, often utilizing over-the-counter pain relievers or opioids. Moderate pain management addresses more persistent discomfort, typically involving stronger prescription analgesics or regional anesthesia techniques. Severe pain management is geared towards patients experiencing intense pain, often requiring advanced interventions like nerve blocks or patient-controlled analgesia systems. Each type is tailored to the patient's pain intensity and recovery needs.</p></p>
