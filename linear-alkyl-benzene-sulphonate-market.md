<p><p>The Linear Alkyl Benzene Sulphonate (LABS) market is divided into two main types: Hard and Soft Linear Alkyl Benzene Sulphonate. Hard LABS typically have longer alkyl chain lengths, providing superior detergent properties and stability, making them suitable for heavy-duty cleaning applications. In contrast, Soft LABS feature shorter alkyl chains, resulting in gentler surfactant properties ideal for personal care and household products. The differentiation allows manufacturers to tailor formulation strategies based on specific cleaning, environmental, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
