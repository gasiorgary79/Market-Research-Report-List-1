<p><p>Elemental analyzers are classified into three market types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual analyzers require operator input for sample preparation and data analysis, offering greater control but increased labor. Semi-automatic analyzers streamline certain processes to enhance efficiency while still needing some manual intervention. Fully automatic analyzers provide a complete automated solution, handling sample loading, data processing, and reporting, which reduces human error and increases throughput, making them ideal for high-volume testing environments.</p></p>
