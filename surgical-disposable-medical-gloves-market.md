<p><p>The surgical disposable medical gloves market includes several types: latex, nitrile rubber, PVC, and polyurethane. Latex gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort but can cause allergic reactions. Nitrile rubber gloves are durable, puncture-resistant, and hypoallergenic, making them a popular choice. PVC gloves are economical and provide basic protection but lack the tactile sensitivity of latex. Polyurethane gloves are soft and flexible, offering enhanced comfort and sensitivity, often used in situations requiring high dexterity. Each type caters to specific needs in medical settings.</p></p>
