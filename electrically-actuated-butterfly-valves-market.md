<p><p>Electrically actuated butterfly valves are essential in controlling fluid flow in various industries. The wafer type is designed for installation between two flanges, offering a compact and lightweight solution, ideal for pipelines with limited space. Conversely, the lug type features threaded inserts, enabling bolted connections on both sides, providing flexibility for maintenance and installation in systems where valves need to be removed without disrupting piping. Both types enhance operational efficiency and facilitate automated control in fluid handling applications.</p></p>
