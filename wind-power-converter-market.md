<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double-fed converter</li><li>Full-power converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power converter market primarily consists of two types: double-fed converters and full-power converters. Double-fed converters use both rotor and grid-side power electronics, allowing only partial conversion of wind energy, which enhances efficiency and provides grid support. In contrast, full-power converters convert all generated power, offering better grid stability and control. Both types cater to different turbine designs and operational needs, influencing overall performance, cost, and adaptability in various wind energy applications.</p></p>
