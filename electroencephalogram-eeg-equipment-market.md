<p><p>The Electroencephalogram (EEG) equipment market comprises two primary types: EEG caps and EEG computers. EEG caps are specialized headgear embedded with electrodes that capture electrical activity in the brain, ensuring comfort and facilitating easy application during tests. EEG computers, on the other hand, are the systems that process, analyze, and visualize the data collected from the caps, providing healthcare professionals with critical insights into brain function and disorders. Together, these components enhance diagnostic accuracy in neurology.</p></p>
