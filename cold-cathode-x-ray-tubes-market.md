<p><p>Cold Cathode X-ray Tubes are specialized devices that generate X-rays using a cold cathode electron emission process. Unlike traditional X-ray tubes, which rely on heated filaments, cold cathode tubes utilize a phenomenon called field emission to produce electrons, resulting in more efficient and stable operation at lower power requirements. This technology is particularly beneficial in medical imaging, industrial radiography, and security scanning applications due to its enhanced image quality and reduced energy consumption.</p><p>The Cold Cathode X-ray Tubes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period.
