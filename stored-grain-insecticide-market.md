<p><p>The stored grain insecticide market consists of several types, each serving specific pest control needs. Organophosphates effectively disrupt pest nervous systems but can have environmental concerns. Pyrethroids provide quick knockdown effects against insects. Methyl carbamates are potent and target a range of pests. Neonicotinoids act on the central nervous system of insects with lower toxicity to non-target species. Bio-insecticides, derived from natural materials, promote eco-friendliness. Other products include various synthetic and traditional compounds catering to diverse storage environments.</p></p>
