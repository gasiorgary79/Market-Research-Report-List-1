<p><p>The portable metal detectors market is categorized into three primary types: Pulse Induction (PI), Beat Frequency Oscillation (BFO), and Others. Pulse Induction detectors use a single coil to emit short bursts of current, effectively detecting deeper metals and providing superior performance in challenging environments. Beat Frequency Oscillation detectors utilize two coils to generate a continuous signal, ideal for shallow finds. The "Others" category includes hybrid technologies and specialized models designed for specific applications, such as treasure hunting or security.</p></p>
