<p><p>The Digital Reading Pipettes market is divided into two main types: Single-Channel and Multi-Channel pipettes. Single-Channel pipettes are designed for precise measurement of individual samples, ideal for applications requiring accuracy and control in small volumes. In contrast, Multi-Channel pipettes allow simultaneous dispensing across multiple samples, greatly enhancing efficiency in high-throughput settings like laboratories. Both types offer digital displays and programmable features for improved user convenience, but their selection depends on the specific needs of experimental workflows.</p></p>
