<p><strong>The Orthopedic Surgery Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Surgical Navigation Systems</li><li>Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Systems</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic surgery navigation systems enhance the precision of surgical procedures. Optical surgical navigation systems utilize cameras and markers to track instruments and patient anatomy in real-time, providing visual guidance during complex surgeries. Electromagnetic surgical navigation systems, on the other hand, use magnetic fields to determine the position of instruments, offering an alternative for tracking without line-of-sight limitations. Other market types may include hybrid systems that combine features of both optical and electromagnetic methods, as well as advanced technologies like intraoperative imaging.</p></p>
