<p><p>The emergency generators market comprises primarily two types: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are versatile, easily transportable units that provide temporary power during outages or outdoor activities, making them ideal for camping and small-scale emergencies. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed systems that automatically kick in during power failures, ensuring continuous power supply for homes or businesses. Both types serve critical roles in maintaining power availability, with portable generators offering flexibility and standby generators providing reliability.</p></p>
