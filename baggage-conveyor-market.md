<p><ul><li>Conveyor Belt</li><li>Conveyor Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baggage conveyor market is primarily divided into two types: conveyor belts and conveyor rollers. Conveyor belts are continuous loops of material that transport luggage efficiently, often used in airports for check-in and baggage reclaim. Conveyor rollers, on the other hand, consist of cylindrical rollers that facilitate movement, typically offering better load handling and flexibility in design. Both systems are essential for optimizing the flow of baggage, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing handling times in transportation hubs.</p></p>
