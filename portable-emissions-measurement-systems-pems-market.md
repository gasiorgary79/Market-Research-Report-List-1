The Real-Driving Emissions (RDE) PEMS focuses on capturing emissions in typical driving scenarios, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. Integrated PEMS (iPEMS) combines traditional lab-based testing with real-world data collection, offering a comprehensive solution for emissions analysis. Both types enable regulators and manufacturers to assess and improve vehicle performance, ultimately supporting environmental objectives and public health.</p></p>
