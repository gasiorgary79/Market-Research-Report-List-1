<p><p>The Airport Lighting Solutions market encompasses various essential components for safe aircraft navigation. Airport Beacons signal the location of airports, while Visual Glide Slope Indicators and Visual Approach Slope Indicators (VASI) guide pilots on approach. Precision Approach Path Indicators (PAPI) provide precise altitude information. Runway Lighting and Runway Edge Lighting ensure visibility during landings and takeoffs, whereas Taxiway Lighting assists in safe taxiing. Obstruction Lighting alerts pilots to hazards near the airport, enhancing overall safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
