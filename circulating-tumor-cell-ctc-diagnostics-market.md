<p><strong>The Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CTC Enrichment</li><li>CTC Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) diagnostics market encompasses two main types: CTC enrichment and CTC detection. CTC enrichment involves isolating tumor cells from blood samples, enhancing the sensitivity of subsequent analyses. Techniques like filtration, density gradient centrifugation, and immunomagnetic separation are used for enrichment. On the other hand, CTC detection focuses on identifying and quantifying these enriched tumor cells through methods like PCR, fluorescence imaging, and high-throughput sequencing, providing insights into cancer progression and treatment response.</p></p>
