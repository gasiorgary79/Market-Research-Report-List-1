<p><p>The benchtop color spectrophotometer market is segmented into various types, including double-beam and single-beam configurations, along with other specialized models. Double-beam spectrophotometers offer enhanced precision by comparing the sample with a reference beam, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate measurements. Single-beam spectrophotometers, on the other hand, measure the light passing through the sample alone, often used for routine analysis. Other models may include advanced features like multiple wavelengths or integrated software for specific applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
