<p><p>The hot forging lubricants market is divided into two main types: graphite-based and non-graphite-based forging lubricants. Graphite-based lubricants are valued for their high thermal stability and excellent lubricating properties, making them suitable for extreme temperatures and heavy loads. Non-graphite-based lubricants encompass a variety of formulations, including water-based and mineral oil-based options, offering unique advantages like reduced environmental impact and versatility. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing tool life, improving product quality, and reducing friction during the forging process.</p></p>
