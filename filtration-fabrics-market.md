<p><p>Filtration fabrics are essential in various applications and are categorized into different types based on their weave structure. Monolayer weaves consist of a single layer of fabric, offering straightforward filtration solutions. Double weaves feature two intertwined layers, providing enhanced strength and filtration efficiency. Three-layer weaves combine three fabric layers for superior filtration, durability, and flow rate control. "Other" market types include specialized weaves or composite materials designed for specific filtration needs, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
