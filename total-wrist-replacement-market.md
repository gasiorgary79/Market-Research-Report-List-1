<p><p>The Total Wrist Replacement (TWR) market involves surgical procedures where damaged wrist joints are replaced with artificial components, aimed at restoring functionality and alleviating pain. Total Wrist Fusion (TWF), on the other hand, entails fusing the bones of the wrist to eliminate movement, thereby providing stability and pain relief. Both procedures target conditions like arthritis, with TWR offering mobility and TWF emphasizing strength and reduced pain. The choice between them depends on the patient's specific needs and overall wrist condition.</p></p>
